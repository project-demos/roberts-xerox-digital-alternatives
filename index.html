Key Features

We identified the main reasons people interact with documents and developed a digital alternative that allows you to do these things without printing paper. We call it Xerox® Digital Alternatives. Check it out.

Key Features | Read and Review

View any file type on either PCs or iPads. Xerox® Digital Alternatives automatically synchronizes across your devices and marks your place as you go, so when you reopen a document you pick up on the exact page you left off.

Key Features | Annotate and Comment

Xerox® Digital Alternatives allows you to mark up digital documents with highlights, circles, sketches and text. The ease and familiarity of paper, with the security and integration of digital.

Key Features | Complete and Sign a Form

Signing contracts and other important documents on the go has long been a challenge for mobile workers. With Xerox® Digital Alternatives, intelligent form field recognition and digital signature support make completing forms quick and easy.

Key Features | Share

Documents are designed to hold information and meant to be shared. Xerox® Digital Alternatives allows you to quickly and easily share documents with team members, meeting attendees or an entire department. Perfect for instantly distributing meeting notes and document markups with a work group.
